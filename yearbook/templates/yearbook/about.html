Why a yearbook? Why Autbase?

"It's been a long day without you, my friend
And I'll tell you all about it when I see you again"

I still remember the first time I came to the CE department of AUT. I look back on those memories with fondness and a happy heart, as I know many of us do and will continue to do. This website exists for that very reason: to help us remember who we were when we first joined CE@AUT and to remind us of the growth and changes we've experienced, shaping us into the people we are now as we leave.

  • If you are an AUT CE graduate (Class of '98), I invite you to create an account and reminisce with your classmates about the days we shared.

  • If you are a recent graduate from any other AUT department, email me, and I will create a space for your class on the website.

  • If you want to create a yearbook like this one for your own graduating class, you can access the source code on my GitHub repo.

  • If you enjoyed the site, I kindly ask you to give a star to the repo. It means a lot to me.

Lastly, this yearbook was made and is now hosted on a server that I have rented with my own pocket money. If you liked the site and are in a position to help, please contact me. As of now, I am uncertain about the site‚Äôs future stability due to financial constraints.
